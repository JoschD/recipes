ingredients:
- 5 eggs 
- 180g sugar
- 1Pack vanilla sugar
- 250g quark 
- 200g flour 
- 200g ground nuts (e.g. almonds) 
- juice of one orange
- 1 pack baking powder
- handfull of raisins


directions:
- Beat eggs + sugar until foamy/creamy. 
- Spoon quark under the egg mixture.
- Slowly add flour and nuts
- Slowly add rest
- Add into greased form and bake at 180Â° (convection) for 50min
