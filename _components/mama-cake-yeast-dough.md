category: dough

# image: käsekuchen_filling.jpg

# prepTime: 30 minutes
# cookTime: 0 minutes
# totalTime: 30 minutes


ingredients:
- 800g Flour
- 1 Block of Yeast
- 2 Pack Vanilla Sugar
- 1 Pinch of Salt
- 1 Egg
- 150g Butter
- 250ml Milk

directions:
- Melt butter.
- Warm milk.
- Mix ingredients.
- Bake for min at 180°C (circulating heat).
