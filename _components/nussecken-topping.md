ingredients:
- 180 butter (or margarine)
- 3 Tbsp. water
- 2 Tbsp. cream
- 200g sugar
- 2 pack vanillesugar
- 300-400g crushed hazelnuts (preferrably pre-roasted!)
- 200g ground hazelnuts (or almonds)

directions:
- Heat butter and water slowly in a pot.
- Mix all other ingredients into pot
- Let it cool down.
