ingredients:
 - 200g Sugar 
 - 3 Tblsp. Starch
 - 600g cream cheese
 - 200g Quark light
 - 150g Cream
 - 1 Egg
 - 2 Tblsp. lemon juice

directions:
- Mix sugar, starch, cream cheese, quark until creamy.
- Add egg, cream and lemon juice and mix **by hand** until smooth.
